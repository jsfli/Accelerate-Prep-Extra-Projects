Let's Play

Rules:

Ping Pong will count up to your number from 1 with the following exception:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 become "Ping"
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 become "Pong"
  3. Numbers divisible by 3 and 5 become "Ping-Pong"
